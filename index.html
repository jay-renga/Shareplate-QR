Our Future Plans

Daily Surplus Collection 
        Work with supermarkets to collect unsold but fresh food (like bread, fruits, dairy) every day before it gets thrown away.
       The SharePlate Van can pick it up at closing time.
        This food can go straight to shelters, schools, or families in need.

Launch a Mobile App
        We plan to release a Share Plate app on the App Store and Google Play so more people can donate or receive food from their phones.

Smart Notifications
      Add a feature to alert nearby users when food is available in their area – quick and helpful!

Multilingual Support
        Add more language options in the app so people from different backgrounds can use it easily.
     
Eco-Friendly Packaging
         Introduce biodegradable packaging for donated food to keep the environment safe while helping people.

Involve Local Restaurants
         Encourage more restaurants and cafes to donate extra food daily, especially during closing hours.
